About Me

Hello! My name is Alexandrea Flint and I have recently started my web development journey. Six months ago, I moved from Buffalo, New York to Layton, Utah for a promotion that allowed me to move to a place where I could further enjoy the outdoors. I moved out here in May and haven't looked back since. In my free time, I am an active individual and love all things outside such as hiking, biking, camping, skiing and snowboarding.

I have been a Project Manager for software upgrades and implementations for just about three years now and recently made a change from our Aerospace and Defense group to our Medical Devices group. I am hoping that by taking the University of Utah Coding Boot Camp that I can further my technical knowledge and advance the skill set that I offer to both myself and my team. My goal at the end of this course is to either become a Technical Project Manager, or pursue a career in Web Development or Software Engineering.
